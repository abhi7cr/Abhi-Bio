Abhi | Dance Bio

About

Abhi is a passionate and versatile dancer with a rich background as both performer and instructor.

• University Dance Team member (2009–2011), with multiple solo and team wins at inter-college level.
• Performed at the IPL Cricket opening ceremony in Chennai.
• Stage performer for artists Sean Paul, Akcent, and Flo Rida during their India tours.
• Hip Hop Dance Instructor at Hip Hop India, Bangalore (2012–2015).
• Currently teaching free dance classes at Amazon (2024–present).
• Trained in Hip Hop, House, Popping, and Locking styles.
• Former member of school and university soccer teams; zonal-level soccer & solo winner.

Specialties

  • House
  • Popping
  • Locking
  • Stage Performance / Competition Prep
